Frequently Asked Questions

Is the app available on iOS?

Currently, VetAssistant is exclusively available for Android devices. We are considering an iOS version for the future based on user demand.

How is my data stored and is it secure?

All your data is securely stored on Google's Firestore, a robust and industry-standard cloud database. This ensures your data is safe, private, and synced in real-time.

Can I customize the skill checklist?

The initial version uses a standardized list of 74 core skills to ensure a comprehensive baseline for all clinics. We are actively working on adding customization features in a future update.

What is the pricing model?

VetAssistant is free to download for both veterinarians and assistants. The core features for training one assistant are free. We will be introducing a simple, affordable subscription for managing multiple assistants soon.
